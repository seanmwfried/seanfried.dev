To the English Side.

About Me

My name is Sean Matthew Wenzel Fried, and I'm 31 years old!

I suppose one could say I was introduced to computers was when I was 3 years of age and played Space Harrier 2 for the Sega Genesis for the first time (I beat it the third time I played, by the way, but I was 4 by then. #brag), but the first time I ever sat down in front of a computer and learned the DOS commands to get me to Dig Dug was at the age of 8.

I continued to learn about computers throughout my youth, including taking as many computer classes my high school offered. I then proceeded to college to study Business Technology & Networking, with which I currently have an Associates. With school finished, I decided to work in the food industry while focusing on playing music and spent almost a decade touring across the United States performing songs I had written.

At the beginning of 2017, I was approached by a friend to build a website for the company for which they worked. I had very limited knowledge at the time, but I knew that I could learn whatever I needed. While working on the project, I realized that I absolutely loved what I was doing, so I started NOLA Web Hosting with a friend to continue working with small, local businesses to provide them with affordable websites and hosting, and so I could continue gaining experience and work towards shifting my career to web development.

Now, 2 years later, I am confident in my foundation and am reaching out to bigger and better technologies and opportunities to become a valuable asset to a company through which I can continue to grow. I wholeheartedly appreciate your interest in me and look forward to speaking with you! :]

自己紹介

１９８８年３月８日に生まれで
３１歳のフリード・ショーンです。

セガのマスターシステムはパソコンと言えるか分かりませんが、３歳で初めてスペースハリアー２というゲームをプレイしました。パソコンの前に座って、ディグダグをプレイさせるＤＯＳのコマンドを覚える時は８歳でした。

パソコンの勉強は小学校、中学校、高校でも続いてて、大学の専門はネットワークの構築で、準学士を手に入れて卒業しました。
卒業後、飲食料品工業に入って、音楽のキャリアを集中しながら１０年間ツアーであったり私が書いた歌を演奏しました。

２０１７年の始まりに、友達に友達が働いていた会社のウェブサイトを建てることを頼まれました。その時、プログラミングが出来ませんでしたが、自分で学べると信じました。途中で、プログラミングが好きだと理解が出来ました。もっとプログラミングをしたくて、こんな業界に転業が出来るように、ノーラー・ウェブ・ホスティングと言うホスティングサービス会社を開業しました。

２年が過ぎた現在は、自身を持ってて、色々なテクノロジー、フレームワークと、大事な社員に成れて開発者として私の成長が出来る会社に入社することを目指しています。私に興味があって、このウェブサイトをご覧頂き、誠にありがとうございます！宜しくお願いいたします！

Rock
Guitar

My father is a bassist, and my mother plays acoustic. I suppose it's only natural that I picked up playing guitar as well. Starting at the age of 12, I've focused mostly on rhythm and songwriting. No shredding here. Most of my guitars have been Schecters, with my current one being a Hellraiser C-1 EX Baritone.

Rock
Singing

My mother is a multi-instrumentalist, but her most powerful instrument is definitely her voice. Luckily, a small bit of her talent was passed down to me. I've been singing all my life and starting singing in bands when I was 18 years old.

MUSIC
EDM
Future Bass

It's only been a few years since I've started listening to Future Bass, but it's quickly become my favorite EDM genre. From creating tunes to listening to artists, it's something I can't live without now.

EDM
FL Studio

I started playing around with FL Studio when it was still called Fruityloops. I've used it for everything from writing demos for my bands to creating EDM songs. Currently, I use it most to make Future Bass.
